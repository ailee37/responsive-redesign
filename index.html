Reimagining Aspara Asian Restaurant

Aspara Asian Restaurant serves a vast variety of Asian cuisines ranging from Chinese to Cambodian. It began as a small food catering business for the Rhode Island community and expanded int the brick-and-mortar shop it is today.

Aspara as an establishment is known for their cozy atmosphere and delicious yet reasonably priced dishes. The owners display their Cambodian roots proudly through their many menu offerings and colorful decor which is why I was taken aback when I first saw their website. The current scheme and layout do nothing to show Aspara for what it really is. Their food isn't showcased, and the text looks a bit cluttered, making this the perfect candidate for a potential redesign.

⮟

Aspara Asian's Current Website

The current website doesn't display any indicator that Aspara is a food based business. From first impression I've gathered, the big image of the dancers' hands along with the color scheme leads people to believe this is a message parlor as opposed to a restaurant.

The current exterior and interior of Aspara look like this, so I wanted to take inspiration and transfer those vibes into the new redesign.

With this in mind, I knew there were a few key details I wanted to highlight in my redesign: emphasis on food and a new color scheme to reflect a more authentic experience of Aspara as a restaurant.

Redesigned Home Page

Key features included:

  • A Green, Red, and Gold color scheme
  • Menu Preview to showcase more food
  • An Order Button for quick access
  • Interactive Map to help users locate the restaurant more easily

Key changes made:

  • Removed most background information (moved to "About" tab)
  • Made text more emphasized (ex: hours is now much larger)
  • Removed one of the maps (redundant)
  • More navbar tabs for better organization
